Rules

Rock-paper-scissors is a simple hand game played between two participants. In this case it's between you and the computer. The rules are straightforward: rock beats scissors by smashing them, scissors beat paper by cutting it, and paper beats rock by wrapping around it. If both players choose the same move, the round results in a tie. The game can be played in a single round or as a series, with the winner determined by who wins the most rounds.
